Avocent Port Renamer - How to Use


1. Ensure you are connected to the VPN on which your Avocent is located.

2. Enter the information relevant to your Avocent.

3. Click 'Modify Names' and enter your list of desired hostnames.

4. Port renaming will always start on port 1.

5. Save and close the document.

6. Click 'Rename Ports' and the process will begin.
